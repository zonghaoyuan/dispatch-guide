## What is Dispatch?

**Dispatch** is an episodic narrative adventure game where you play as **Robert Robertson III** (Mecha Man), coordinating a team of reformed villains through the Superhero Dispatch Network. Released in 2025 for PlayStation 5 and PC, Dispatch combines choice-driven storytelling with strategic hero management.
