## Episode Overview

Episode 3 "Turnover" is the most critical episode for story branching. It contains the **permanent romance decision** that cannot be changed and determines which endings are available in Episode 8.

Approximately 1-2 hours into Episode 3, during a pivotal character interaction scene.

### Your Three Options

**Option 1: Romance Blonde Blazer**
- **Locks You Into**: Blonde Blazer romance ending path
- **Ending Access**: Romance Blonde Blazer + Identity variants
- **Relationship Type**: Professional becomes personal, power couple
- **Requirements**: Relationship 7+ recommended

- **Locks You Into**: Team Unity OR Solo Leader paths

- **Best For**: Players focused on gameplay or non-romantic endings

## Story Guide

### Coupe's Past Emerges

**The Trust Question**

Coupe's former crew is rumored to be active. Team questions Coupe's loyalty.

**CHOICE: Trust Coupe**

**Option A: Trust Him**
- **Impact**: Coupe remains loyal and effective
- **Relationship**: +10 with Coupe
- **Missions**: Coupe available for all missions
- **Story**: Team unity strengthened

**Option B: Doubt Him**
- **Impact**: Coupe becomes distant
- **Relationship**: -10 with Coupe
- **Missions**: Coupe less effective, may refuse some missions
- **Story**: Team tension increases

> **Recommendation**: Trust Coupe for best outcomes

### Torrance Pier Incident

**Major Emergency**: Multiple civilians at risk, high-stakes scenario

**Strategy Choice**

**Aggressive Assault**:
- Higher risk, faster resolution
- Potential civilian casualties
- Requires Combat 5+ heroes
- Success Rate: 70-80%

**Tactical Approach**:
- Safer, slower resolution
- Minimal casualties
- Requires Intellect 4+ and Combat 4+
- Success Rate: 85-95%

> **Recommendation**: Tactical Approach with Invisigal + Malevola + Punch Up

## Missions Guide

### Mission Difficulty Spike

Episode 3 missions require **stats 4-5** minimum for success.

### Mission 1-2: Standard Emergencies
**Requirements**: Combat 4, Charisma 3
**Recommended**: Prism + Invisigal
**Success Rate**: 85%+

### Mission 3: Torrance Pier (Critical)
**Requirements**: Combat 5, Intellect 4, Vigor 4
**Recommended**: Invisigal (Combat), Malevola (Int), Punch Up (Vigor)
**Success Rate**: 90%+ with full team
**Failure Consequences**: Civilian deaths, team morale loss

### Mission 4-7: Simultaneous Emergencies

**First Major Test**: 4-5 missions at once

**Priority System**:
1. **High Priority**: Civilian lives, story missions
2. **Medium Priority**: Property damage, standard emergencies
3. **Low Priority**: Minor incidents (acceptable to fail)

**Rotation Strategy**:
- Deploy Tier 1 heroes to high priority
- Deploy Tier 2 heroes to medium priority
- Use Phenomenon for low priority (2-second cooldown)
- Accept 1-2 failures if necessary
