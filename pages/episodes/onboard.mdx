## Episode Overview

Episode 2 "Onboard" introduces the real Z-Team members and increases gameplay complexity. Robert continues adapting to his dispatcher role while building relationships and facing more challenging missions.

---

## Key Story Moments

### The Real Z-Team Introduction

**Heroes Unlocked**:
- **Invisigal** (Courtney) - Former villain, combat specialist
- **Prism** - Illusion master, versatile hero
- **Malevola** - Intellect and charisma specialist
- **Sonar** - Transformation abilities, support role
- **Punch Up** - Unkillable tank
- **Coupe** - Former assassin, precision striker

### The Twinks Incident

**Situation**: Robert accidentally crushes his Twinks (in-game snack)

**CRITICAL CHOICE: How to Handle It**

**Option A: Chastise Sonar**
- **Impact**: Establishes strict, authoritative leadership
- **Relationship**: -5 with Sonar
- **Leadership Style**: Boss-focused
- **Future**: More professional boundaries

**Option B: Get More Twinks (Laugh It Off)**
- **Impact**: Shows flexibility and humor
- **Relationship**: +5 with Sonar, +3 team morale
- **Leadership Style**: Friend-focused
- **Future**: More casual team dynamics

> **Recommendation**: Get More Twinks for better team morale and relationships

## Missions Guide

### Mission 1: Armed Robbery
**Requirements**: Combat 3, Mobility 2
**Recommended**: Invisigal (Combat 4, Mobility 3)
**Strategy**: Solo deployment
**Success Rate**: 90%+

### Mission 2: Hostage Situation
**Requirements**: Charisma 4, Intellect 2
**Recommended**: Prism (Charisma 4) + Malevola (Intellect 4)
**Strategy**: Dual deployment for coverage
**Success Rate**: 85-95%

### Mission 3: Technical Emergency
**Requirements**: Intellect 4, Vigor 2
**Recommended**: Malevola (Intellect 4)
**Strategy**: Solo with backup option
**Success Rate**: 80-90%

### Mission 4: Monster Attack
**Requirements**: Combat 3, Vigor 3, Charisma 3
**Recommended**: Punch Up (Vigor 4, Combat 3) + Prism (Charisma 4)
**Strategy**: Tank + support combo
**Success Rate**: 90%+

### Mission 5-6: Simultaneous Emergencies
**First Time**: You'll face 2-3 simultaneous missions
**Strategy**:
- Prioritize high-casualty missions
- Use your developed roster rotation
- Accept possible failure on lowest priority

## Strategy Guide

### Hero Leveling Priority (Episode 2)

**Must Level**:
1. **Invisigal**: Combat to 5 (XP farming with Wolf Pack)
2. **Prism**: Charisma to 5 (versatility)
3. **Malevola**: Intellect to 5 (technical missions)

**Secondary Priority**:
4. **Punch Up**: Vigor to 5 (tank)
5. **Coupe**: Combat to 5 (backup DPS)

### XP Farming Strategy

**Invisigal Wolf Pack Abuse**:
- Deploy Invisigal SOLO on easy combat missions
- Wolf Pack doubles XP for entire team
- Best missions: Armed robbery, simple combat
- Can level team 1-2 levels this episode

### Cooldown Management

**Episode 2 Rotation**:
- **Tier 1**: Invisigal, Prism, Malevola (primary)
- **Tier 2**: Punch Up, Coupe, Sonar (secondary)
- Keep 6 heroes in active rotation
- Never deploy fatigued heroes (40%+)
