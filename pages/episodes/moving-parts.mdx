## Episode Overview

Episode 6 "Moving Parts" is the second most critical episode. It contains the **Mecha Man identity revelation choice** that determines which ending variants you can access.

---

## üö® THE PERMANENT IDENTITY CHOICE üö®

### When It Happens
Mid-episode, during a crisis where your Mecha Man identity becomes relevant.

### Your Two Options

**Option 1: Reveal Identity**
- **Publicly announce** you are Mecha Man
- **Ending Access**: All "Identity Reveal" ending variants
- **Impact**: Transparency, public heroism, media attention
- **Team Reaction**: Mixed - some appreciate honesty, others worried
- **Public**: Increased support and scrutiny
- **Future**: Can't operate anonymously

**Option 2: Keep Secret**
- **Maintain separation** between Robert and Mecha Man
- **Ending Access**: All "Secret Keeper" ending variants
- **Impact**: Mystery, protected anonymity, subtle influence
- **Team Reaction**: Only select members know truth
- **Public**: Can operate freely without legacy burden
- **Future**: Flexibility in identity management

### Ending Combinations

| Base Ending | + Reveal | + Secret |
|-------------|----------|----------|
| Romance Blonde Blazer | Public power couple | Private power couple |
| Romance Invisigal | Public redemption story | Secret love story |
| Team Unity | Public legendary team | Underground legend |
| Solo Leader | Public tactical genius | Mysterious mastermind |

## Story Guide

### The Mecha Man Mystery Deepens

**Revelations About the Suit**:
- Family legacy implications
- Technological secrets
- Why it malfunctioned

**Robert's Internal Struggle**:
- Accept legacy or forge own path?
- Public hero or shadow operator?
- Honor family or protect team?

### Alliance Decisions

**Multiple Factions Approach You**

**Faction A: Official Hero Association**
- **Alliance**: Government support, resources, restrictions
- **Rejection**: Independence, less support, more freedom

**Faction B: Independent Hero Network**
- **Alliance**: Flexibility, community, less resources
- **Rejection**: Isolation, self-reliant

> **Recommendation**: Choose based on your playstyle and ending path

### Team Trust Decisions

**How Much to Share**

**Full Transparency**:
- Tell team everything about Mecha Man, plans, struggles
- **Impact**: +20 team trust, vulnerability, unity
- **Risk**: Information could leak
- **Best For**: Team Unity path

**Strategic Secrecy**:
- Share on need-to-know basis
- **Impact**: +10 team efficiency, professional
- **Risk**: Trust issues if discovered
- **Best For**: Solo Leader path

### Highest Difficulty Missions Yet

**Requirements**: Stats 6-7 minimum
**Complexity**: Multi-stage operations
**Stakes**: Hero casualties possible

### Mission Types

**Mission 1-3**: Crisis Convergence
- Multiple threats simultaneously
- **Requirements**: Full roster deployment
- **Strategy**: Deploy all 3 waves

**Mission 4-5**: Identity-Related Missions

- **May require specific heroes**

**Mission 6-9**: The Chaos Cascade
- 6-7 simultaneous emergencies
- **Absolute peak of dispatch challenge**

- Public mission as Mecha Man
