## Critical Choices

### Leadership Philosophy: Friend vs Boss

**THE DEFINING CHOICE**

**Option A: Friend**
- Position yourself as teammate and friend
- **Impact**: +15 team morale, casual dynamics
- **Ending Path**: Team Unity more likely
- **Relationships**: Closer bonds, informal atmosphere
- **Pros**: Better team chemistry, higher synergy bonuses
- **Cons**: Less authoritative control

**Option B: Boss**
- Maintain professional superior position
- **Impact**: +10 respect, professional boundaries
- **Ending Path**: Solo Leader more likely
- **Relationships**: Respectful distance, formal structure
- **Pros**: Clear command structure, tactical efficiency
- **Cons**: Lower team intimacy

**Option C: Balanced**
- Mix of friendship and authority
- **Impact**: +10 morale, +5 respect
- **Ending Path**: Either Unity or Solo viable
- **Relationships**: Professional friendship
- **Recommended**: Best overall approach

### Ethical Approach to Missions

**Methods Question**: How far will you go to succeed?

**Option A: By Any Means Necessary**
- Ruthless tactics, results-focused
- **Impact**: Higher mission success, lower civilian trust
- **Team**: Some heroes uncomfortable
- **Future**: Aggressive options available

**Option B: Principled Approach**
- Moral constraints, ethical methods
- **Impact**: Some mission failures, higher civilian trust
- **Team**: Heroes respect your integrity
- **Future**: Diplomatic solutions preferred

> **Recommendation**: Principled Approach for Team Unity path, flexible for Solo Leader

### Team Unity vs Individual Needs

**Resource Allocation**

**Scenario**: Limited resources - help struggling hero or maximize team effectiveness?

**Option A: Help Struggling Hero** (Individual Focus)
- Shows care for each member
- **Impact**: +10 with that hero, team sees compassion
- **Ending**: Supports Team Unity

**Option B: Maximize Team Effectiveness** (Utilitarian)
- Invest in strongest heroes
- **Impact**: Better mission results, struggling hero feels neglected
- **Ending**: Supports Solo Leader

### Relationship Optimization

**For Team Unity Ending**:
- Build ALL relationships to 8+
- Deploy varied teams (rotate heroes together)
- Choose collaborative dialogue
- Prioritize team over individual glory

**For Solo Leader Ending**:
- Focus on mission efficiency
- Deploy optimal teams regardless of relationships
- Choose authoritative dialogue
- Prioritize results over feelings
