# Episode 1: Pivot - Complete Walkthrough

![Episode 1 Pivot - Mecha Man Cockpit](/images/Mechaman_cockpit.webp)

**Release Date**: October 22, 2025

## Episode Overview

Episode 1 "Pivot" serves as the introduction to Dispatch, where you experience Robert Robertson III's transition from active superhero Mecha Man to dispatcher. This episode teaches core game mechanics while establishing key relationships and your leadership style.

---

## Part 1: Opening Sequence - The Goon Interrogation

![Mecha Man Combat](/images/Mechaman_punch.webp)

### The Hanging Goon Scene

**Situation**: You're confronting a Soothing Goon, hanging him off a building to extract information about Shroud's location.

**CHOICE 1: The Goon's Fate**

**Option A: Let Him Drop**
- **Result**: Goon survives via convenient mattress below
- **Impact**: Establishes ruthless, fear-based reputation
- **Long-term**: Opens aggressive dialogue options in future episodes
- **Character Perception**: Blonde Blazer and civilians see you as intimidating
- **Recommended For**: Players wanting aggressive/pragmatic Robert

**Option B: Pull Him Back**
- **Result**: You show mercy and restraint
- **Impact**: Builds compassionate reputation
- **Long-term**: Better civilian relations, more diplomatic options
- **Character Perception**: Blonde Blazer respects your morality
- **Recommended For**: Players wanting heroic/moral Robert

> **Guide Recommendation**: Pull Him Back for best long-term relationships and romance options.

## Part 2: Combat Sequence

### Hacking Mini-Game

**Objective**: Navigate data squares and open paths

**Instructions**:
1. Move right across the screen
2. Press buttons as they appear (timing based)
3. Open path to first data square
4. Return and open path to second data square
5. Access remaining data squares

**Tips**:
- Higher Intellect stat makes this easier
- Failed hacks can be retried
- Take your time - speed isn't critical here

### Confronting Toxic

**Boss Fight Preparation**

**Dialogue Choices** (minimal impact):
- "It's over, Shroud."
- "Prepare to die."
- "I'm here to f*** you up."

**CHOICE 2: Finishing Move**

**Option A: PUNT**
- **Result**: Spectacular, cartoonish moment where Toxic flies across room
- **Impact**: Purely visual/comedic
- **Memorable for**: Flashy players

**Option B: STOMP**
- **Result**: Grounded, brutal finishing move
- **Impact**: Purely visual/dramatic
- **Memorable for**: Serious players

> **Note**: This choice is cosmetic only - choose based on your preferred style.

### Power Diversion Sequence

**QTE Section**

Toxic warns that diverting pulse power won't work.

**Dialogue Options** (no mechanical impact):
- "Bullsh*t."
- "IGNORE HIM."
- "You're bluffing."

> **Note**: Choose any option - all lead to same cutscene outcome.

## Part 4: Robber Encounter

### Blonde Blazer Rescue

**Situation**: Robbers beat you until Blonde Blazer arrives

**CHOICE 3: Accept Help**

When Blonde Blazer offers to fix your arm:
- **Accept Help**: Shows willingness to rely on others (builds Blonde Blazer relationship)
- **Refuse Help**: Demonstrates independence (affects perception)

> **Romance Path**: Accepting help starts positive dynamic with Blonde Blazer.

### Combat Choice

**CHOICE 4: Punch Direction**

**Option A: PUNCH WITH LEFT**
- **Result**: Solid hit, effective combat
- **Impact**: Tactical efficiency
- **Recommended**: Yes - better combat outcome

**Option B: PUNCH WITH RIGHT**
- **Result**: Less effective approach
- **Impact**: Suboptimal but viable

> **Combat Tip**: Left hand punch is mechanically superior.

## Part 5: Dispatcher Training

![Mecha Man Cockpit View](/images/Mechaman_cockpit.webp)

### Training Heroes

**Important**: The 6 heroes in training segment:
- Do NOT have abilities yet
- Do NOT gain experience from training missions
- This is purely tutorial

### Mission 1: Chase Scene

**Requirements**: Speed + Combat

**Recommended Team**:
- **Pom Pom** (High Mobility)
- **Equilibrium** (Combat support)

**Strategy**: Deploy both for guaranteed success

**Success Rate**: 85-95% with recommended team

### Mission 2: Hospital Attack

**Requirements**: High Vigor + Combat

**Recommended Hero**:
- **Phenomaman** (Best for endurance + combat)

**Strategy**: Solo deployment works well

**Success Rate**: 80-90% with Phenomaman

### Mission 3: Monster Attack

**Requirements**: Team coordination, Vigor + Charisma

**Recommended Team**:
- **Pom Pom** (Coordination)

- **Brainbook** (Vigor for endurance)

**Strategy**: Full 3-hero deployment

**Success Rate**: 90-95% with full team

## Learning Objectives

### Core Mechanics Introduced

1. **Hero Stats**: Understanding Combat, Vigor, Charisma, Mobility, Intellect
2. **Mission Matching**: Matching hero strengths to mission requirements
3. **Timer Management**: Making quick decisions under pressure
4. **Success Probability**: Using the simulator before deployment

### Key Gameplay Lessons

**Stat Matching**:
- Combat → Fighting missions
- Mobility → Chase/speed missions
- Vigor → Endurance/dangerous missions
- Charisma → Social/crowd control
- Intellect → Technical/hacking

**Team Deployment**:
- Solo for simple missions
- 2-hero for medium complexity
- 3-hero for high-risk missions

## Episode 1 Strategy Guide

### Optimal Choices for Best Outcomes

1. **Goon Interrogation**: Pull Him Back (compassion)
2. **Toxic Fight**: Either option (cosmetic)
3. **Power Diversion**: Any option (no impact)
4. **Reporter Interview**: Confident answers (strong image)
5. **Accept Blonde Blazer's Help**: Yes (relationship building)
6. **Combat Punch**: Left Hand (tactical efficiency)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Gameplay Mistakes

1. **Ignoring Stat Requirements**: Don't send Combat heroes to Charisma missions
2. **Solo on Monster Attack**: Always use full team for this training mission
3. **Rushing Decisions**: Take time to understand hero stats

### Story Mistakes

1. **Inconsistent Personality**: Mixing compassionate and ruthless randomly
2. **Ignoring Relationships**: Not building foundation for future romances
3. **Skipping Dialogue**: Missing important character development
