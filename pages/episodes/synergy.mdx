## Episode Overview

Episode 8 "Synergy" is the finale where all narrative threads conclude. Your cumulative choices determine which of six endings you experience, with quality variants based on your performance.

---

## The Six Endings

### 1. Romance: Blonde Blazer

**Unlocked By**:
- Episode 3: Romance Blonde Blazer
- Episode 6: Any identity choice
- Episodes 1-8: High relationship (9-10)

**Variants**:
- **+ Reveal**: Public power couple leading SDN
- **+ Secret**: Private partnership, mysterious power

**Finale Focus**: Robert and Blonde Blazer's partnership

**Epilogue**: Future as couple and leaders

---

### 2. Romance: Invisigal

**Unlocked By**:
- Episode 3: Romance Invisigal
- Episode 6: Any identity choice
- Episodes 1-8: High relationship (9-10), support her redemption

**Variants**:
- **+ Reveal**: Public redemption story, reformed love
- **+ Secret**: Hidden romance, protected from scrutiny

**Finale Focus**: Robert and Invisigal's trust and redemption

**Epilogue**: Future together, her hero status secured

---

### 3. Team Unity

**Unlocked By**:
- Episode 3: No romance
- Episode 5: Friend leadership style
- Episodes 1-8: High team morale (average 8+)

**Variants**:
- **+ Reveal**: Public legendary team
- **+ Secret**: Underground legend, mysterious unit

**Finale Focus**: Z-Team as unified force

**Epilogue**: Team's legendary status, collective achievement

---

### 4. Solo Leader

**Unlocked By**:
- Episode 3: No romance
- Episode 5: Boss leadership style
- Episodes 1-8: High mission success, tactical excellence

**Variants**:
- **+ Reveal**: Public tactical genius, renowned strategist
- **+ Secret**: Mysterious mastermind, shadow operator

**Finale Focus**: Robert's individual excellence

**Epilogue**: Robert as legendary leader/tactician

---

### 5. Mecha Man Identity Reveal

### 6. Secret Keeper

## Mission Strategy

### For Perfect Ending (Best Variant)

**Requirements**:
- 95%+ success rate on all finale missions
- All heroes alive and ready
- Maximum relationships (9-10)
- Optimal stats (7+)

**Strategy**:
1. Deploy absolute best teams
2. Over-deploy (use 3 heroes when 2 might work)
3. Use Prism duplication on best heroes
4. Have Punch Up as safety net
5. Fresh heroes only (0% fatigue)

### Team Compositions

**For Critical Missions**:
- **Ultimate Combat**: Invisigal (7), Coupe (6), Punch Up (7)
- **Social Perfection**: Prism (7), Malevola (7), Sonar (6)
- **Balanced Power**: Prism (7), Invisigal (7), Malevola (7)
