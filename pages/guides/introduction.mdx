# Introduction to Dispatch

![Dispatch Game Banner](https://shared.akamai.steamstatic.com/store_item_assets/steam/apps/2592160/header.jpg)

## What is Dispatch?

**Dispatch** is an episodic superhero workplace adventure game developed by AdHoc Studio, released between October and November 2025 on PlayStation 5 and Windows. Created by former Telltale Games developers, Dispatch combines narrative-driven storytelling with strategic hero management in a unique superhero universe.

## Game Overview

![Dispatch Gameplay](https://img.youtube.com/vi/G52jvfcGmGo/maxresdefault.jpg)

In Dispatch, you play as **Robert Robertson III**, formerly known as the superhero **Mecha Man**. After losing the ability to fight crime directly due to issues with his inherited Mecha Man suit, Robert transitions from frontline hero to dispatcher, coordinating a team of rehabilitated villains-turned-heroes through the **Superhero Dispatch Network (SDN)**.

The game blends:
- **Choice-based narrative** with meaningful consequences
- **Strategic hero management** and mission deployment
- **Workplace comedy** mixed with dramatic superhero action
- **Character-driven storytelling** featuring a star-studded voice cast

## Key Features

### Episodic Structure
Dispatch is released in eight episodes over four weeks:
- **Episodes 1-2 (Pivot & Onboard)**: October 22, 2025
- **Episodes 3-4 (Escalation & Priority)**: October 29, 2025
- **Episodes 5-6 (Team Building & Moving Parts)**: November 5, 2025
- **Episodes 7-8 (Retrospective & Synergy)**: November 12, 2025

### Core Gameplay Elements

1. **Strategic Dispatch System**: Assign heroes to emergencies based on their stats, abilities, and cooldowns
2. **Choice-Driven Narrative**: Every decision shapes relationships, story direction, and endings
3. **Hero Management**: Level up and customize your team of reformed villains
4. **Quick Time Events (QTEs)**: Engage in timed sequences during critical moments
5. **Hacking Mini-Games**: Navigate data paths and disable threats
6. **Multiple Endings**: Six different endings based on your choices throughout the game

## The Z-Team

You'll manage a diverse roster of former villains who have joined the Phoenix Program to become heroes. Each member has unique abilities, stats, and personalities that affect both gameplay and story. Your job is to:
- Deploy the right heroes for each emergency
- Manage their cooldowns and fatigue
- Build relationships and team synergy
- Make strategic decisions that impact mission success

## What Makes Dispatch Special?

Dispatch stands out by combining:
- **Meaningful Choices**: Decisions have lasting consequences on relationships and story outcomes
- **Strategic Depth**: Hero deployment requires careful consideration of stats, abilities, and team composition
- **Character Development**: Rich character arcs with humor, drama, and emotional depth
- **Accessibility**: Engaging for both strategy enthusiasts and narrative-focused players

## Critical Reception

Since its release, Dispatch has received strong critical acclaim for its:
- Compelling storytelling and character depth
- Meaningful player choices and branching narratives
- Strategic gameplay that complements the narrative
- High production values and voice acting
- Unique blend of workplace comedy and superhero action

The game has been praised as a potential Game of the Year contender, with near-perfect scores from critics and strong player reception.
