## Overview of Endings

**⚠️ MAJOR SPOILERS - This guide reveals all ending paths ⚠️**

Dispatch features **six distinct endings** in Episode 8 (Synergy), determined by your choices throughout all eight episodes. The two most critical decision points are:

1. **Episode 3**: Romance choice (permanent and cannot be changed)
2. **Episode 6**: Identity revelation choice

Your cumulative choices throughout Episodes 1-8 also affect which ending variant you unlock and the quality of that ending.

---

## The Six Endings

### 1. Romance Ending: Blonde Blazer

**Type**: Romance / Partnership

**Theme**: Power, Partnership, Professional-Personal Balance

#### Requirements

**Critical Choices**:
- **Episode 3**: Choose to romance Blonde Blazer (PERMANENT CHOICE)
- **Episodes 4-8**: Maintain and build relationship with Blonde Blazer
- **Relationship Level**: High (8+ positive interactions)

**Supporting Choices**:
- Professional yet personal dialogue choices
- Support Blonde Blazer's leadership decisions
- Balance work and personal life
- Demonstrate competence and partnership equality

#### Path to This Ending

**Episodes 1-2**:
- Build positive relationship with Blonde Blazer
- Respectful professional interactions
- Show competence in dispatcher role
- Supportive dialogue choices

**Episode 3** (CRITICAL):
- **Choose Blonde Blazer romance option**
- This locks you into romance path
- Cannot be reversed after this episode

**Episodes 4-6**:
- Nurture relationship through dialogue
- Support her decisions
- Complete missions successfully (professional competence)
- Choose partnership-focused options
- Balance authority with intimacy

**Episodes 7-8**:
- Maintain high relationship
- Make choices supporting partnership dynamic
- Complete Blonde Blazer specific missions
- Choose "together" options in finale

#### Ending Description

Robert and Blonde Blazer acknowledge their romantic relationship while maintaining professional excellence. They become a power couple in the superhero world, balancing leadership roles with personal connection. The Z-Team respects and supports their relationship.

**Epilogue**: Shows Robert and Blonde Blazer leading the SDN together, combining professional achievement with personal happiness.

#### Best For

Players who enjoy:
- Romance with authority figures
- Power couple dynamics
- Professional success with personal fulfillment
- Blonde Blazer's character

## Ending Quality Tiers

Each ending has quality variants based on performance:

### Perfect Ending (Best Variant)
**Requirements**:
- 90%+ mission success rate
- High relationship scores (relevant to ending)
- Optimal choices throughout episodes
- All key missions completed successfully

**Result**: Best epilogue, most satisfying conclusion, all characters thrive

### Good Ending (Standard Variant)
**Requirements**:
- 70-90% mission success rate
- Moderate relationship scores
- Most key choices optimal
- Some mission failures acceptable

**Result**: Satisfying conclusion, most characters happy, minor complications

### Acceptable Ending (Lower Variant)
**Requirements**:
- 50-70% mission success rate
- Low relationship scores
- Suboptimal choices
- Multiple mission failures

**Result**: Bittersweet conclusion, some characters dissatisfied, complications

### Poor Ending (Worst Variant)
**Requirements**:
- Below 50% mission success rate
- Very low relationships
- Many poor choices
- Significant failures

**Result**: Unsatisfying conclusion, unhappy characters, major complications

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my Episode 3 romance choice later?**
A: No, it is permanent. You must replay from Episode 3 or start new game.

**Q: Can I get multiple endings in one playthrough?**
A: No, one ending per playthrough. Choices lock you into specific paths.

**Q: Which ending is "best"?**
A: All endings are valid. "Best" depends on your preference and playstyle.

**Q: Do I need perfect missions for perfect ending?**
A: 90%+ recommended but not 100% required. Some failures acceptable.

**Q: Can I romance both characters?**
A: No, must choose one or neither in Episode 3.

**Q: Does Identity choice in Episode 6 give different endings?**
A: Yes, it creates variants of your base ending (Romance/Unity/Solo).

**Q: How many playthroughs for all endings?**
A: Minimum 3, recommended 4 for all variants.

**Q: Can I miss an ending permanently?**
A: No, you can always replay to get any ending.
