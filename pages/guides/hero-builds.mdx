## Understanding Hero Builds

A hero build refers to how you allocate stat points and develop abilities as your heroes level up. The right build can transform a decent hero into an exceptional one for specific roles and mission types.

## Build Philosophy

### Specialist vs. Generalist

**Specialist Builds**:
- Focus on 1-2 primary stats
- Excel at specific mission types
- Higher success rates in their niche
- Vulnerable when deployed outside specialty

**Generalist Builds**:
- Distribute points across 3-4 stats
- Handle various mission types adequately
- Flexible deployment options
- Lower peak performance but more versatile

**Recommendation**: Build 60% specialists, 40% generalists for optimal roster flexibility.

## Common Build Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Over-Generalizing

**Problem**: Spreading stats too thin across all heroes

**Solution**: Build clear specialists with defined roles

**Example**: Don't try to make Invisigal good at Charisma - embrace her social weakness

### Mistake 2: Neglecting Core Team

**Problem**: Leveling too many heroes equally in early game

**Solution**: Focus on 3-4 core heroes first, then expand

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Abilities

**Problem**: Building stats without considering hero abilities

**Solution**: Build around abilities - e.g., Invisigal's Wolf Pack benefits from solo viability

### Mistake 4: No Backups

**Problem**: Only having one hero per role

**Solution**: Build at least two heroes per primary stat for cooldown rotation

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Team Chemistry

**Problem**: Building heroes who don't work well together

**Solution**: Consider relationship bonuses and team synergies

## Final Build Tips

1. **Start with a Plan**: Know your endgame build before spending points
2. **Synergy First**: Build around hero abilities and team composition
3. **Cover Weaknesses**: Ensure team has no glaring stat gaps
4. **Test Regularly**: Use simulator to validate build choices
5. **Stay Flexible**: Be ready to adapt based on mission demands
6. **Document Success**: Track what works for future playthroughs
7. **Enjoy Experimentation**: Try different builds to find your style

The perfect build balances specialization with flexibility, creating a roster capable of handling any mission while supporting your preferred playstyle and story choices.
